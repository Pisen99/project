About

Borgafjäll is a small Sami village up north in Sweden with only 74 residents.
This pristine gem isolated among beautiful mounitains offers one of the best off-pist routes in Sweden.
All though the village may seem small it is a nichc ski lover spot that does feature a varaity of restaurants and accommodation.
You could choose to live in the famous hotel off the architect Ralph Erskine or right under Borgafjäll's own "little" goliath, Borgahällan.

Activities

Borgafjäll Summer Season

Fishing: A lot of fishing-spots around the rivers with different fishes like “rainbow-fishes” or “Abborre”.
Mountain-bike: When the snow is gone, the slopes turn in to an mountain-bike hill. Easy or more extreme tracks.
Hiking: In Borgafjall you have a variety of hiking options, either family tracks or more extreme routes.

Borgafjäll Winter Season

Cross-skiing: The tracks are 10, 5 and 2,5km and goes right under Borgahallan or up on the mountains.
Snowmobiles: Every restaurant in Borgafjall has rentals for this.You could take a day trip up on the sunny mountain along with a hot chocolate.
Slopes: Borgfjall offers one of Swedens best off-pist routes for professionals or regular post for families. There are around 11 ski-routes right now, winter 2022 there will be 18 ski-routes.

Dining

Skicenter

They can offer you a great variety of food. Accommodation in their own apartments. The restaurant`s position lays in the center of the slopes. Renting the equipment you need to go out in the slopes. When night falls this restaurant also offers a night club with different events from time to time.

Borgafjäll Hotell

Is famous for it`s architecture in Sweden. The hotel was drawn by Ralph Erskine 1950,
with the inspiration of the mounitains surrounding Borgafjäll. This hotel can offer their visitors spa and relaxation. A good dining is an option too along with their famous whine seller.

Borgagården

The restaurant is surrounded by panorama windows
with the view of Borgafjäll`s very own mountain Borgahällan. This place offers you fine-dining on another level. A saloon in the basement for people who love cowboys and dressing up. For you that wants to stay here, they have 13 cabins for their visitors.
