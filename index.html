About

Borgafjäll is a small Sami village up north in Sweden with only 74 residents.
This pristine gem isolated among beautiful mounitains offers one of the best off-pist routes in Sweden.
All though the village may seem small it is a nichc ski lover spot that does feature a varaity of restaurants and accommodation.
You could choose to live in the famous hotel off the architect Ralph Erskine or right under Borgafjäll's own "little" goliath, Borgahällan.

Dining

Skicenter

They can offer you a great variety of food. Accommodation in their own apartments. The restaurant`s position lays in the center of the slopes. Renting the equipment you need to go out in the slopes. When night falls this restaurant also offers a night club with different events from time to time.

Borgafjäll Hotell

Is famous for it`s architecture in Sweden. The hotel was drawn by Ralph Erskine 1950,
with the inspiration of the mounitains surrounding Borgafjäll. This hotel can offer their visitors spa and relaxation. A good dining is an option too along with their famous whine seller.

Borgagarden

The restaurant is surrounded by panorama windows
with the view of Borgafjäll`s very own mountain Borgahällan. This place offers you fine-dining on another level. A saloon in the basement for people who love cowboys and dressing up. If you want to stay here they have 13 cabins for their visitors.
